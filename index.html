THE FILM PRODUCTION COMPANIES

Today, the Big Three majors – Universal Pictures, Warner Bros. Pictures, and Columbia Pictures – dominated the global film industry and made approximately 45 billions in the box office history.

Being a leader in film entertainment, Universal Pictures has released more than 300 titles. In 2015, the highest-grossing movie created by Universal to date brought in a crazy $1.6 billion worldwide.

Columbia Pictures is a film production studio that is a member of the Sony Pictures Motion Picture Group. The Columbia Pictures becomes one of the major film companies in the world with the its popular titles: Ghostbusters II (1989), Spider-Man(2002), Venom(2018), Jumanji:The Next Level(2019).

Warner Bros. Pictures is a film production company founded by brothers Harry Warner, Albert Warner, Sam Warner, and Jack L. Warner. In 2011, the company produced the highest-grossing film in the Harry Potter franchisee, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2 with $1,342,222,791 worldwide.
